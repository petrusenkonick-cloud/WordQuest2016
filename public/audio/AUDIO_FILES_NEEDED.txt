WORDQUEST AUDIO FILES NEEDED
=============================

Download free audio from these sources:
- Kenney.nl: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds (CC0 - free)
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/music/ (Royalty-free)
- Mixkit: https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/ (Free)

SOUND EFFECTS (put in /public/audio/sfx/)
-----------------------------------------
click.mp3          - Button click sound (~50ms, short click/tap)
correct.mp3        - Correct answer sound (~200ms, positive chime)
wrong.mp3          - Wrong answer sound (~300ms, error buzz)
level-complete.mp3 - Level complete fanfare (~2s, celebratory)
achievement.mp3    - Achievement unlocked (~1.5s, special)
reward.mp3         - Coin/reward collect (~300ms, bling)
daily-reward.mp3   - Daily reward claim (~1s, special chime)
hint.mp3           - Hint reveal (~200ms, soft notification)
transition.mp3     - Page/screen transition (~150ms, whoosh)
countdown.mp3      - Timer tick (~100ms, tick sound)

BACKGROUND MUSIC (put in /public/audio/music/)
----------------------------------------------
menu-theme.mp3     - Main menu music (calm, magical, ~2-3 min loop)
game-loop.mp3      - In-game music (light, not distracting, ~2-3 min loop)
boss-battle.mp3    - Boss level music (exciting, tense, ~2-3 min loop)
victory.mp3        - Victory music (triumphant, ~5-10 seconds)

RECOMMENDED FREE PACKS:
1. Kenney Interface Sounds: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds
   - Perfect for UI clicks, correct/wrong sounds
   - CC0 license (completely free, no attribution needed)

2. Kenney Music Jingles: https://kenney.nl/assets/music-jingles
   - Short victory/level complete sounds
   - CC0 license

3. Pixabay Game Music:
   - Search "game music" or "fantasy game"
   - Filter by "Royalty-free"
   - Good for background loops

FILE FORMAT:
- MP3 format preferred (smaller file size)
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps is sufficient
- Keep files small for fast loading

NOTES:
- Audio system will gracefully handle missing files
- Files will be preloaded on app start
- Mobile browsers require user interaction before playing audio
